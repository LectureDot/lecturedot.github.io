Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LectureDot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and the LectureDocs suite of assessment tools, including LectureSeats, LectureTest, and LectureAssign.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At LectureDot, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We understand the importance of keeping your personal information confidential and secure.

Information We Collect

When you use LectureDot and LectureDocs, we may collect information necessary to provide our services. This includes, but may not be limited to:

  • Account Information: Information you provide when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and institutional affiliation.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our services, including interactions with LectureSeats, LectureTest, and LectureAssign. This may include attendance records, exam submissions, assignment progress, and other related data.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device and internet connection, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Optional Data: If you choose to use features like selfies in LectureSeats, those images will be collected for the purpose of attendance verification.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services, including the functionality of LectureSeats, LectureTest, and LectureAssign.
  • Verify identity and facilitate secure access to our tools.
  • Support educational activities, such as tracking attendance, grading assignments, and administering exams.
  • Conduct research to enhance the effectiveness of our educational tools and practices.
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

Data Security and Sharing

We will never sell your personal data to any third party.

Your data may be used for educational and research purposes within the scope of higher education. This means your information might contribute to studies aimed at improving teaching and learning methodologies or the effectiveness of educational technology. However, we guarantee that no identifying information will ever be made public by LectureDot in connection with any such research or educational use. All data used for research will be anonymized or aggregated to ensure your personal identity is protected.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services (e.g., cloud hosting providers), but only to the extent necessary for them to perform their functions and always under strict confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with our legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Please contact us if you have any questions or requests regarding your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the Effective Date located at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the methods provided on our website.
